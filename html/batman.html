Description

During his second year of fighting crime, Batman pursued the Riddler, a sadistic serial killer who targeted political figures and elite citizens of Gotham City. Batman attempts to uncover hidden corruption in the city and questions his family's involvement. He connects with his own family during the investigation, and is forced to make new allies to catch the Riddler and bring the corrupt to justice. Batman also has to forge new relationships to unmask the culprit. He seeks to bring justice to the abuse of power and corruption that has long plagued the metropolis.
