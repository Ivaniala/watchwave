Description

Captain Yoo Si-Jin (Song Joong-ki) and Sergeant Major Seo Dae-young (Jin Goo) are trying to catch a motorbike thief named Kim Gi-beom. Because he suffered injuries when he fell from his motorbike, the thief was taken by two soldiers to Haesung Hospital, where Kang Mo-yeon (Song Hye-kyo) works as a doctor. Haesung Hospital is the place where Si-jin and Mo-yeon meet for the first time. Si-jin is fascinated by Mo-yeon's character and beauty. Unfortunately, Mo-yeon couldn't just trust these two soldiers. Moreover, Si-jin has the nickname "Big Boss" which is synonymous with gangster leaders. This made Mo-yeon suspicious that the two people (soldiers) were actually ganging up on her patient.
