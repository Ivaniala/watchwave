Description

Miles Morales (Shameik Moore) tries to live a normal life in his universe and tries to hide his dual identity as Spider-Man. One day, Miles meets someone from another universe named Jonathan Ohnn aka The Spot, who has the power to explore the universe. Miles also meets Gwen Stacy (Hailee Steinfeld), aka Spider Woman, who visits him and takes him to explore the universe. However, Miles clashes with Miguel and the Spider Society over how to deal with the threat. A conflict occurs that forces Miles to be faced with two difficult choices: saving the people he loves or saving the entire universe.
